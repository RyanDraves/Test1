The Science Squad


About Us:

Hello! Welcome to the Science Squad! We are a nonprofit made for youth, by youth, that promotes scientific inquiry and the pursuit of STEM careers in Western Michigan. Our goal is to engage the youth of Western Michigan in activities that will better prepare our generation for the scientific and technological advances that loom before us. We host events such as science fairs and summer camps, as well as presentations on scientific breakthroughs and misconceptions. Every event that we host is completely free. To view some of our programs, check the tab above, or to fulfill daily dose of science, head over to our YouTube Channel, where we host a live lecture series with professionals, or our Facebook, where we often share entertaining science articles and videos.

The Team

Arya Rao

Arya Rao is currently a junior at Big Rapids High School and founded The Science Squad in 2016. She has been involved in research programs at the University of Chicago, and will return next summer to work in a faculty lab. She has a passion for the process of scientific inquiry, and hopes to spread her love of the sciences to the youth of Western Michigan by encouraging scientific exploration.

Susan McCullen

Susan McCullen is currently an AP Biology teacher in Big Rapids, Michigan. She is a devoted mother of two sons and a daughter. As a grader of AP Biology exams, she witnesses thousands of students' passions for science, and wants to spread that interest to Western Michigan.

Tyler O'Dell

Tyler O'Dell is a dedicated science educator in Big Rapids, Michigan. Originally from Saline, Michigan Tyle graduated from Lake Superior State University before earning advanced degrees in chemistry and education from Washington State University and Western Kentucky University. Tyler is an avid outdoors man and enjoys the natural world both as an academic and as a sportsman.
