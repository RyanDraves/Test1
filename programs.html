Programs

Sign up for the Mecosta Osceola Science Symposium 2017!

Have some fun in our first annual science fair! This will be the first science fair in the area to embrace team competition in science fairs, so be sure to be a part of the first of many awesome science fairs! Entry is completely free, and prizes will be awarded for outstanding competitors.


Register for the Lecture Series!

Whether you're prepping for MOSS or just want to learn more about science, the Lecture Series is a great way to prepare yourself for your future in science! Here, you'll be able to interact with highly educated science professionals who will introduce you to both innovative STEM concepts and post-secondary education plans for STEM majors. These professionals will also coach you if you wish as you prepare for the MOSS competition, or any other science fair. Register below, and go to our YouTube channel to attend the lectures!
